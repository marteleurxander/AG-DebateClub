About Us

Our vision is to let students teach students through reasoned debate and diverse perspectives. We aim to build confidence in public speaking, structured argumentation, and critical thinking.

We meet weekly for a mix of traditional debates, skill-building exercises, and collaborative discussions that help members grow both socially and intellectually.

Our goals include preparing members for events such as the MIC school debates, speech competitions, and helping them develop skills that extend beyond school into university and professional life.

Meetings

We meet in the Debate Hall (Floor 3) every Thursday at 15:30, unless otherwise announced.
