About Us

We aim to build confidence in public speaking, structured argumentation, and critical thinking through regular debate and discussion.

Our sessions combine traditional debates, exercises, and collaborative learning designed to help members grow academically and socially.

We prepare students for MIC school debates, speech competitions, and future university or professional environments.
