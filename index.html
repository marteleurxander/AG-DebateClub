About Us

Our vision is to let students teach students through reasoned debate and diverse perspectives. We focus on building confidence in public speaking, structured argumentation, and critical thinking.

We meet weekly for debates, skill exercises, and open discussions. The goal is simple: to grow together through discussion and curiosity.

We also prepare members for MIC school debates, speech events, and future academic or professional environments.

Debate Hall (Floor 3), every Thursday at 15:30 unless otherwise stated.
