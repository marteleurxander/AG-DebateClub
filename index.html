About Us

We aim to build the confidence, public speaking ability, and critical thinking of our members through regular debate, discussion, and the broadening of perspective.

We prepare our members for the MIC school Debates, speech competitions, and how to convince and be convinced by others.

Our sessions include traditional debates, exercises, lessons, and opportunities to get to know one another.
