About Us

Our vision is to let students teach students through reasoned debate and diverse perspectives. We aim to build confidence in public speaking, structured argumentation, and critical thinking.

We meet weekly for debates, skill exercises, and open discussions that encourage curiosity and growth.

Our goal is to prepare members for MIC school debates, speech competitions, and future academic or professional life.

Debate Hall (Floor 3), every Thursday at 15:30 unless otherwise stated.
